## 👨‍🏫 Academic Profile

I am a motivated Computer Science educator and researcher with experience in undergraduate teaching, software development, academic coordination, and student mentoring. I am currently serving as a **Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering** at **Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST)**.

My current research focus is in **Computer Vision**, with particular interest in **Person Re-Identification, Video Understanding, Violence Detection, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning**. I am passionate about delivering quality education, guiding students in programming and research, and contributing to technology-driven problem solving through impactful computer vision research.
